Our Story

A Legacy of Comfort & Nature

Welcome to MTDC Holiday Resort

Nestled near the iconic Aakashganga Sky Observatory, MTDC Resort Matheran offers a perfect blend of tranquility and colonial charm. For over 50 years, we have been the preferred choice for travelers seeking a peaceful retreat in the heart of the Sahyadris.

Our resort is surrounded by dense evergreen forests, offering direct access to nature trails and scenic viewpoints. Whether you are here for a romantic honeymoon, a family vacation, or a quiet solo trip, our warm hospitality ensures a memorable stay.

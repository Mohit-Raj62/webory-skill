Welcome to
MTDC Resort Matheran

Nestled amidst the serene beauty of the Sahyadri hills, MTDC Resort Matheran stands as a beacon of comfort and heritage. Strategically located opposite the iconic Aakashganga Sky Observatory, our resort offers a unique blend of colonial charm and modern amenities, making it the best hotel in Matheran for nature lovers.

Steps away from popular viewpoints like Louisa Point and Charlotte Lake, our property provides direct access to dense forest trails. Whether you are planning a romantic honeymoon, a family vacation, or a solo retreat, our eco-friendly cottages ensure a peaceful stay away from the city's chaos. Experience the "Cutest Little Hill Station of Asia" with the warm hospitality of Maharashtrian culture at MTDC.

A Feast for Your Senses: At MTDC Resort Matheran, dining is not just a meal; it’s an experience. Our open-air restaurant offers a serene ambiance where you can enjoy your food under a canopy of trees or starry skies. We specialize in authentic Maharashtrian Thalis, zesty Pithla Bhakri, and refreshing Solkadhi, alongside a wide range of North Indian, Chinese, and Continental dishes.
